About

The Golden Ore Bank has been specializing in personalized banking and accounting services throughout the Mumbai area since 2010. In addition to our certified accountants, we have the skills, knowledge and charisma to handle all of your accounting needs. We are ready to tackle any financial challenges that you may have as your trusted tax planner and financial advisor.

  • 2010-2013

    Our Foundation era

    The Golden Ore Bank was established on 5th Aug 2010 having its headquarter at Mumbai. Intially we started selling financial services to large and mid-size companies, SMEs and retail businesses

  • May 2014

    Expansion

    We started providing banking services all over India. We opened 10 new branches in Gujrat, Panjab, UP, Karnatata and Delhi

  • December 2015

    Transition to Full Service

    From Dec 2015, we started providing fullscale financial services like Investment Banking, Global Investment Research, BFSI etc.

  • Phase Two Expansion 2019-2022

    In Nov 2019 we opened our first overseas branch in USA. The same year we opened an office in London, UK. In 2020, we opened a branch in the Dubai International Financial Centre and branches in Hong Kong. And we are still growing!

Our Investment Banking teams deliver high-quality strategic advice and creative financing solutions to our clients, including mergers and acquisitions, financing, and risk management transactions.

We pride ourselves in our resourcefulness and work on a variety of initiatives. On any given day, our work could include advising a company on a cross-border merger, structuring the initial public offering of a subsidiary, refinancing an outstanding bond and more. As a division, our strategic objectives include: To be the world’s preeminent investment bank – trusted advisor, financier and risk manager for our clients on their most important transactions Build long-term relationships with clients and bring them world-class execution by “delivering the firm” over time Drive superior returns for our stakeholders Be the employer of choice for all career levels by providing a highly dynamic, meritocratic, diverse and rewarding workplace
